My Favourite Books

  • Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone by J.K. Rowling

    • Description of the book...

    This story is about a young boy, Harry Potter, who discovers that he is a wizard. He joins a magical school called Hogwarts and makes new friends. Together, they face challenges and uncover secrets about his past.

  • The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

    • Description of the book...

    This book is about a young shepherd named Santiago who goes on a journey to find treasure. Along the way, he learns valuable lessons about life, dreams, and the importance of following one's heart.

  • Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki

    • Description of the book...

    This book contrasts the financial philosophies of two father figures in the author's life: his own father (poor dad) and his friend's father (rich dad). It emphasizes the importance of financial literacy and investing.

  • Atomic Habits by James Clear

    • Description of the book...

    This book provides a practical framework for building good habits and breaking bad ones. It emphasizes the power of small, incremental changes and how they can lead to significant improvements over time.

  • The Power of Your Subconscious Mind by Joseph Murphy

    • Description of the book...

    This book explores the concept of the subconscious mind and how it can be harnessed to achieve personal success and fulfillment.
