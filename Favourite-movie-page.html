My Favourite Movies

  1. Hackers (1995)

    2. Description of the movie...

    Hackers is a 1995 American crime film directed by Iain Softley. The film follows a group of young hackers who uncover a conspiracy while being pursued by the authorities. It explores themes of cybercrime, technology, and the hacker subculture.

  2. WarGames (1983)

    3. Description of the movie...

    WarGames is a 1983 American science fiction thriller film directed by John Badham. The film follows a young hacker who accidentally accesses a military computer system and nearly triggers a nuclear war.

  3. Blackhat (2015)

    4. Description of the movie...

    Blackhat is a 2015 American thriller film directed by Michael Mann. The film follows a disgraced hacker who is forced to work with the FBI to stop a cyber-attack on a major financial institution.

  4. The Matrix (1999)

    5. Description of the movie...

    The Matrix is a 1999 science fiction action film written and directed by the Wachowskis. The film depicts a dystopian future in which humanity is unknowingly trapped inside a simulated reality created by intelligent machines to distract humans while using their bodies as an energy source.

  5. Tron (1982)

    6. Description of the movie...

    Tron is a 1982 American science fiction film directed by Steven Lisberger. The film follows a computer programmer who is transported into a digital world and must navigate through various challenges to escape.
